About Us

Empowering Lifestyle Support Services is a registered NDIS provider for people with disability, we are committed to and abide by the NDIS Practice Standards and the Quality and Safeguards Commission requirements.

We are committed to empowering our clients to be independent and to continue to be productive members of our society. Our approach to service delivery is based on client centered principles, hence why our clients remain with us and feel like they are part of the Empowering Lifestyle Support Services family. Whether you're looking to enjoy independence in your own home, want to get out and about, learn new skills or need to find someone you rely on for specialists, we're here to help.

Development life skill


Supporting you to develop everyday life skills that increase independence and control.

Empowering Lifestyle Support Services supports you to build skills that are require for everyday life.

We focus on your own specific goals by mastering the skills you need to achieve greater independence, confidence and control.

Our training focuses on practical skills that you can use immediately and in the future, from handling money to travel training, meal preparation, communication, independent living skills or literacy and numeracy.

Training & development support


Empowering Lifestyle Support Services provides High quality Assistance with Daily Living, to support you to live the life you choose.
Our daily living supports are flexible and customised to meet your own needs, goals and outcomes.
We provide support at home, in the community, at school or work. We provide one-on-one support, seven days a week.
You might want:

  • support getting ready for your day
  • to get out and about in the community
  • someone to do household chores
  • someone to help with food preparation and meals
  • assistance getting to appointments, or
  • some travel support.

    Life Stage Transition


    Our Life Stage Transition program includes short- and long-term support that strengthening the participant’s ability to coordinate their supports and focuses on assistance to live at home, community participation, and helping you to coordinate your own support.
    This includes:

  • Individual skills development
  • Assistance with decision making, daily planning, budgeting
  • Support connection

    Personal Activities


    As part of your NDIS Package - Personal Care offers assistance with daily self-care tasks in order to help clients maintain appropriate standards of hygiene and grooming.

    Personal care supports relate to assistance with daily personal activities including assistance with, or supervision of, personal tasks of daily life. For example:

  • Personal hygiene, including showering, bathing, oral hygiene, dressing and grooming
  • Eating and drinking
  • Attending appointments
  • Mobility and transferring, for example moving in and out of bed and on or off the toilet or
  • Application of splints, basic first aid due to injuries sustained as a result of a participant’s disability
  • Showering

    Innov community participation


    With one of our caring staff by your side, we can help you get out and about to participate in social activities, sport or to catch up with friends and family.

    Our staff regularly assist people to join in a wide range of activities and community events including:

  • Accompanying you to the park, beach or lake
  • Assistance with food shopping
  • Participation in sporting activities
  • Regular outings to the cinema or shopping
  • Attending religious or community events
  • Visiting the gym to workout
  • Catching up with or meeting new friends
  • Attending arts, drama or music classes
